Knuth 本身想写关于程序语言设计和实现的书，
但是为了准备实现程序语言过程中所用到的数据结构和算法，
需要写一些关于算法的预备的书，于是就有了这一些列的书。

直到现在，都没回到「程序语言实现」这个主题，甚至还没写到语法解析。

- Volume 1 Fundamental Algorithms, Third Edition (1997)
- Volume 1 Fascicle 1, MMIX: A RISC Computer for the New Millennium (2005)
- Volume 2 Seminumerical Algorithms, Third Edition (1997)
- Volume 3 Sorting and Searching, Second Edition (1998)
- Volume 4A Combinatorial Algorithms, Part 1 (2011)
- Volume 4B Combinatorial Algorithms, Part 2 (2023)
- Volume 4 Fascicle 0, Introduction to Combinatorial Algorithms and Boolean Functions (2008)
- Volume 4 Fascicle 1, Bitwise Tricks & Techniques; Binary Decision Diagrams (2009)
- Volume 4 Fascicle 2, Generating All Tuples and Permutations (2005)
- Volume 4 Fascicle 3, Generating All Combinations and Partitions (2005)
- Volume 4 Fascicle 4, Generating All Trees; History of Combinatorial Generation (2006)
- Volume 4 Fascicle 5, Mathematical Preliminaries Redux; Introduction to Backtracking; Dancing Links (2019)
- Volume 4 Fascicle 6, Satisfiability (2015)
- Volume 5 Syntactic Algorithms (in preparation)
